# StarText
Print input text to star (*) character

# Usage
- Run the console app
- Input a text and number size of text

# Characters
Available Characters :

```
  * * * * *      * * * * * *  * * * * * * *  *  * * * * * *
*           *  *              *              *  *           *
*           *  *              *              *  *           *
* * * * * * *  *              * * * * * * *  *  * * * * * *
*           *  *              *              *  *       *
*           *  *              *              *  *         *
*           *    * * * * * *  * * * * * * *  *  *           *
```
